Why Donate?

AUdoc relies on student and alumni contributions to maintain free healthcare services, stock the campus pharmacy, and upgrade medical equipment. Every taka makes a real difference to your fellow students.

What Your Donation Funds
  • Free medicines for registered students
  • Mental health & counseling sessions
  • First-aid equipment and emergency kits
  • Dental and eye care supplies
  • Annual health screening camps
