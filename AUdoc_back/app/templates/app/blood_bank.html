Why Donate Blood?

Blood is essential for surgeries, trauma care, cancer treatment, and managing chronic diseases. One blood donation can save up to three lives. On campus, we rely on student donors to maintain adequate blood supplies for emergencies.

Donor Eligibility
  • Age: 18–65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin: 12 g/dL or higher
  • Blood pressure: Normal range
  • No major illnesses or infections
Donor Rewards
  • Free health screening
  • Refreshments after donation
  • Donor recognition certificate
  • Priority blood access if needed
  • Campus health points reward
